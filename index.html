Visitor Information

Planning a Visit? Our visiting protocols are reviewed in the line with best clinical practice. Check out the information below:

1. Waiting Room: We are dedicated to offering our patients and visitors comfort. A fantastic waiting room is available where patients can wait for long hours at no cost. Drinking water, Clean toiletry and washroom, and Hygienic bathroom (Ladies and Gents both) facilities are available in our clinic.

2. Parking Zone: There is convenient self-parking adjacent to our Clinic's entrance. Our parking lot has almost 30 visitors parking spaces. The parking facility is totally free.

3. Care for Differently abled Patients: We specially care for Differently abled Patients. Wheel Chair support system, Special supporting staffs are available in our clinic.

4. Online Transaction: For your convenience, we offer a wide variety of payment options, including:

a. MasterCard / Visa (debit or credit),
b. Payment by Cash,

c. Online payment through Paytm, Phonepay, Google pay, etc.

5. Security: Coochbehar Polyclinic is dedicated to providing a safe and secure environment, enabling the delivery of world-class care.

6. Safety: Our Clinic has respiratory hygiene stations at the entrance with masks and hand sanitisers available for patients to use. We are committed to providing a safe and patient-friendly environment in our clinic.
